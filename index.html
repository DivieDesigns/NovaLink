Frequently Asked Questions

What is the installation process?

Out team will contact you to schedule an installation date, and the process typically takes 1-3 days.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, we have the offer for you to upgrade your plan at anytime, with our Terms and Conditions. No hidden charges.

Is there a contract?

Not a contract per say, but there is a refund policy if you are not satisfied with our internet provision. You can cancel within the first 7 days.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments throuh different payment channels as we have users across the globe, these different platforms control how much they charge to make payments through their gateway.

How do I contact customer support?

Our customer support is active 24/7, you can reach out via our website or via mail, our customer support responds very fast.

What is the coverage area?

Our signals covers anywhere in the world as the internet is being provided by satellites, we usually have a downtime of 1 hour a month where we do maintenance.
